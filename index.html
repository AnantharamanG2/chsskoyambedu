About Our School

The Chennai Corporation Higher Secondary School, Koyambedu started in 1935 first as a primary school
and over the years upgraded itself into one of the best higher secondary schools in the government sector
with recorded 100% success in the annual state exams.
Situated amidst the busiest vegetable market and bus terminus in the city, the school has thrived through
all its ecosysytem challenges to be a space of excellent education and hope to the community.
As a result of the fervent effort put in by the teaching and nonteaching staffs led by the HM Mrs. Mrunalini
CHSS Koyambedu is now a place of inclusive and holistic learning.
